Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

The past few years, we've started decorating the inside of the WonderLounge tent. This year, we want to step it up a notch and create a light art piece. We will have a heart that beats to either a typical heartbeat, or we can change the beat to sync with the music. There will be "veins" and "arteries" that flow into/out of the heart, and the flow will be animated by pulses of light or the light changing colors. To make the heart more on theme with the burn, we will add paper mache texas flowers on the the veins/arteries/heart. This art piece will be yet another piece of eye candy for Flipizens to enjoy while hanging out at our camp.

What is your timeline detailing the planning, creation, installment, and leave no trace removal of your project?

Week 1&2: Will cut out the heart structure from plywood and attach chicken wire to structure for 3D look. Week 3: Add paper mache to chicken wire and add white expandable vent hose for veins and arteries. Week 4: Paint paper mache heart Week 5-8: Make flowers for decorating. Week 9&10 will be extra time for unexpected issues. In tandem to the building of the physical heart, we will have a software programmer set up the lights for various sequences and speeds.
