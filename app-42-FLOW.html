Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

This piece celebrates the natural beauty of Austin along with the weird. Austin green space comprises four major bodies of water: Lady Bird Lake, Lake Austin, Lake Travis, and Barton Creek. On the outside of the base pedestal is a wrap around continuous shimmer wall of the 4 connected bodies of water. The interior space will incorporate a dynamic color changing visual art mural showcasing the weirdness that is Austin’s hallmark, in addition to 2D art curated from local Austin mixed media artists. On top of the base pedestal will be a kinetic moving dynamic swirling mass of the beloved Congress Street bats that have become synonymous with Austin. The colorful bats take flight powered by and emulating the joy of the burners riding the merry-go-round.
