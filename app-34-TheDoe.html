Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

For this piece I drew inspiration from childhood memories of crafting paper dolls, my love for nature and forest. I designed a whimsical, large-scale jointed puppet(10-12ft) made from painted plywood. Equipped with wooden poles on the hands and feet, for interactive movement, the puppet invites viewers to interact and play. This puppet has a lot of nostalgia for me, creating dolls and characters as a child and escaping into my daydreams. The hope is that it invokes this same nostalgia into the viewer.

Yes, I have made many large scale mixed media projects. In the past I’ve made projects out of metal and wood. In the most recent years, I’ve been experimenting with costumes, Mostly making costumes out of fabric and found plastic. I think the thing that I’ve learned the most from all of my projects is that you have to be innovative when things don’t go as planned.
