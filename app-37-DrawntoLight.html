37 - Drawn to Light

Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

Drawn to Light is a cozy, creative sanctuary where Flipizens can gather, vibe, and let their artistic energy flow. Centered around a low table surrounded by a plethora of cushioned pillows, this chill zone invites folks to craft decorative church fans—both a cool tool for the Texas heat and a tangible expression of their inner spark—or simply kick back, soak in the mellow ambiance, and bask in the ever-changing dance of light and sound. The heart of this piece is the lighting, alive with motion and connection. It responds to your presence, reflecting the energy of those who enter the space and illuminating the shared creative pulse of the moment. As Flipizens create, their handmade fans will weave into the fabric of the installation, transforming the space into an ever-evolving tapestry of art, intention, and togetherness. Every fan tells a story, every piece adds to the collective energy, and by the end of the event, the installation becomes a beautiful reminder of the community’s shared journey. Why fund Drawn to Light? This installation embodies the soul of Flipside: creativity, connection, and celebration of the here and now. It’s a welcoming, come-as-you-are space that encourages folks to co-create and find harmony in the act of making and being. Whether someone’s crafting, chilling, or just passing through, this installation is a moment of shared magic that everyone can feel a part of. The funding will fuel this vision, providing everything from groovy interactive lighting tech to rad materials for crafting and comfy cushions to sink into. It’s all about creating a space that feels inclusive, intentional, and full of good vibes. Not only a sight to experience, Drawn to Light is an interactive art installation with an open invitation to share, create, and be a part of something luminous and unforgettable.

What is your timeline detailing the planning, creation, installment, and leave no trace removal of your project?

2 Months before Flipside: • Finalize the concept design, determine budget • Build and test a small-scale prototype, including lighting, motor, and crafting components. • Purchase supplies, coordinate transportation, and prep tools for setup. 1 Month before Flipside: • Construct the table, assemble the lighting and motor elements, and create sample fans. • Test the full setup to ensure interactive functionality, battery life and safety. Flipside: • Set up all components onsite, test interactivity, and prepare for flipizens. • Guide flipizens in crafting fans, participate and monitor the installation, and maintain safety. End of event: • Disassemble the installation, pack materials, and clean the site to leave no trace. • Store reusable items, recycle materials, and document the project for future endeavors.
