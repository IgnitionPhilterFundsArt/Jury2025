What is your timeline detailing the planning, creation, installment, and leave no trace removal of your project?

Week 1: We will be using a laser cutter to cut the pieces of lycra stretch fabric Week 2: Sewing the edges of the different triangles so that the fabric pieces will not stretch as much and last longer. On site building: Set Geodesic dome up 1/3 of way- Tie on Lycra fabric that goes on top Set up Geodesic dome 2/3 of way- Hang the lycra that goes in the middle of the walls Set up Geodome fully- Tie on rest of lycra that goes onto the side walls that we havent yet.

I'm a digital artist for the metaverse (VR environment developer), and also a VJ who does everything from pre rendered clips, to real time visuals, also video mashing with random clips I get from TV/movies/nature documentaries.
