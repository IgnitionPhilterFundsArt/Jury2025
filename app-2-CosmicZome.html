Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

The Cosmic Zome is a 13 foot tall, 20 foot diameter, projection-mapped zome that debuted at Flipside 2024. Unlike traditional geodesic domes which are built out of triangles, a zome is a similar structure built from kite shapes instead of triangles. This small twist creates a beautiful geometric shape suggesting sacred geometry, with the top of the zome forming the petals of a flower. The wooden frame is covered with 84 panels, and multiple projectors inside the zome create a 360 degree continuous video wall for visitors to enjoy. Visitors are invited to sit or lay down and look up at the roof of the structure to relax and get lost in the immersive video experience. The version that was brought last year used cheap projector mounts which were unreliable and impacted the uptime of the project. A new mounting system has been designed to resolve this issue so the art piece can be experienced by more people. The funding request also includes money for projector rental. If additional money can be raised (beyond Ignition Philter), the projection rental may be replaced with purchasing 3-4 projectors that are a better fit for the project's requirements (brighter and provide more coverage of the structure for a more immersive experience).
