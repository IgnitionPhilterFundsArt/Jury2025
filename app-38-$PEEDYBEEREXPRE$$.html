Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

This is going to be a slimmed down, revamped experiment to what I brought to Flipside 2023. I now have better power management and won't make it so gosh-darn big. So the initial concept of the $PEEDY BEER EXPRE$$ is that everything at the burn is a gift, except this is a LOAN. I will have more signage up and a smaller storefront. Part of the gift will be to accept and crush people's empties, and to supply a drop point for iced beer that subscribers in the know will be able to access more centrally located than their camps. The game of $PEEDY BEER EXPRE$$ is to waste as much time as possible before giving out the beer. This can be done by intake questionnaire, psychological profiling, palm reading, reaction to various stimuli and recording reactions, so on. This is a hang-out project that will need a staff of at least two people to man the "storefront" and have a small hole for beer. There are NO REPERCUSSIONS for not making good on loaned beer, only fake imagined consequences, but still may have people dropping off cases of beer like they did two years ago. I call it the Beeramid Scheme, and I'll make better signage and fun art that will get the point across. It can work with just a little more training than the Bad Advice Booth, but it is a parody of the Customer Service Experience. Simple, easy, fun.
