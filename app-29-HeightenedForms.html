29 - Heightened Forms

Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

Two larger than life burner fabulous beings facing each other. Perhaps facing each other across cornfield row. 10 feet tall painted plywood cutouts. 2 sided with 2x4s sandwiched between and metal brackets for support. Stainless steel cable guy wires and deep auger anchors for stability. Landscape spotlights and LED strips for lighting. Perhaps a burner fabulous character in a cowboy hat facing off with a fabulous cow on hind legs trying to sneak into the burn. Or perhaps just two fabulous burners in cowboy hats, tutus, etc. Or perhaps two versions of one fabulous being looking at itself. Exact characters tbd, yet to be drawn. Photos of the Vaughn, NM highway art included as part of the inspiration with a Texas burner flair.
