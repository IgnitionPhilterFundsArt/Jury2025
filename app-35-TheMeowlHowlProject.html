Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

10 yrs ago, I spent many hours working on a costume called MeOwl based on these photoshopped pics of owls with cat heads. It rained that entire flip side and having expanding metal wings in lightning is conta-indicated for safety. I got the wings to work on the last day of flipside but most of the costume never came together the way I envisioned it. After this past election I decided that focusing on more art would be the main way to get threw the now. When in Houston I was inspired by the Phoenix Art car .it had the same feather effect I had intended . I was inspired to go back to the MeOwl concept, but not as a costume. I feel there is So much going on in the world that we need to howl about it and Iâ€™m presenting the idea of a Meaowl Howl Project , the vision is ideally is: a 2ft ft animatronic, motion sensor reactive installation. with an owls body and a cats head it would have a motion sensor vocal/component of different kinds of howls. and would invite people it senses to howl as well (similar to last years scream on the effigy). I would like to put it up on a self made cat tree. Where it would be above people heads unnoticeable until people walk near it and hear it's howl sound. eyes light up and wings move. I would be learning a lot taking on this art project, thankfully I know friends who are willing work on this with me. These are the ingredients I am asking for financial help to help create this art piece.
