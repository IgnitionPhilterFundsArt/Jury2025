17 - Deep Fried in the Art of Texas

Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

[we] just did Friday FryDay - fried fair food for the OtherWorld’s Fair for FreezerBurn 2025. The [*****] fried up Jalopeno popper egg rolls, funnel cakes Oreos, corn dogs, and more! During this event we also “Poor Dashed” the fried food across the playa to volunteers and anyone needing food brought to them. Food is an art form and what better way to display that art than deep frying it in oil! We expanded the trailer park with our Flamper experiment at FZB2025. The Flamper was the backdrop for our frying event and also hosted entertaining and unique art. It was a very popular event/art installment that we would like to continue offering at Flipside, but would need extra help with costs. Deep Fried in the Art of Texas *clap*clap*clap* - will have unique offerings of fried food on FryDay (7-9pm), we will also have multiple art pieces that will be incorporated into the theme and event.
