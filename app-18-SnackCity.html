18 - Snack City

Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

Snack City is a snack bar/tent open Thursday-Sunday 24 hours a day, The Graveyard/Halloween Town has brought out to Flipside every year since 2016. Imagine you're walking along with your buddies, you just got done dancing your heart out at one of the sound camps, and you're HUNGRY... don't want to walk all the way back to camp? Flipizens already know, The Graveyard/Halloween Town has snacks for this very moment. For when that midnight snack urge creeps in. And WE LOVE providing this for the community. However, we have seen our camp go from 30 people(biggest year) down to 12 last year... we still bring out the same amount of infrastructure and fun every year. And with dwindling camper numbers, yet our hearts still being all the way in this, we would like to ask for assistance with Snack City for the first time. So we can ensure our community stays fueled even into the wee hours of the morning. One of the images below includes an including people from our camp that is being used with consent from them.
