27 - Soup Cart

Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

Imagine you are hot and tired and the sun is beating down. You hear jingley jangley noises approaching, you look up and see a curious rickshaw bumbling towards you. “Cold Soup!” is called out over the crowd. People begin bumbling forward clutching at their bowls and spoons. What have they today? A cold borscht? An aromatic gazpacho? An unknown creation of dubious origin? Slake your thirst and your hunger and get some needed electrolytes from SOUPCART
