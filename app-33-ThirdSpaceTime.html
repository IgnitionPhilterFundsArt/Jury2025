Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

This project is a projection mapped shade sail comfy space for burners to lie down, zone out, and enjoy the visuals mapped onto all of the shade sails through a single fisheye lens connected to an 7k lumen HD laser projector in the middle of the space. The projection will be visible from inside and outside of the dome like structure. Some sort of meditative audio track will be playing and the visuals will react to it. The projection system will only be active during the nighttime, however the meditative soundtrack will play 24/7 and be a nice shaded space to hang out during the day time.
