4 - Mammatus

Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

Mammatus is a fire art sculpture that consists of a high-volume (relatively) low-pressure flame effect that vents onto the underside of a 10' steel plate. This forms a mesmerizing slow ripple of flames (a flaming version of a mammatus cloud.) The disc is securely suspended at 13' from the peak of a dome-like structure built from EMT. In the spirit of 'graceful degredation' the effect can also be used a a high-volume high-pressure boosh (more effective if wind speeds are too high for the mammatus effect.) Burners can watch and push the button to ignite (from a safe distance.) This effect will require me to bring at least 400lbs of propane and rent an 18' trailer for a week. The combined cost of fuel and trailer is almost $700. I would like to request $350 if available to help offset those costs. I have upwards of $2000 invested in the build of the effect (tanks, plumbing, structure, etc.)
