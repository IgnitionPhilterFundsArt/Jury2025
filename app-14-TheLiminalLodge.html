Description for the Jury: Explain the Project in Full. [This is the description that should explain why the jury should give you the funds so try to answer any questions they might have. Please include some language on how the Flipizens will interact with the piece.]

The Liminal Lodge is an immersive, stylized outpost blending art, function, and mystery to create a versatile community space. Housed in a 15-foot octagonal bell tent, the Lodge serves as a haven for relaxation, discovery, and practical needs. Visitors can access essentials like water and electrolyte refill stations, shade, fans, and a self-service gift market stocked with useful items such as first aid supplies, soaps, and lens wipes. Inspired by Gravity Falls, X-Files, and Meow Wolf, the Lodge features an abandoned outpost and research facility aesthetic, filled with interactive props and a custom-written narrative mystery centered on “The Gravitational Anomaly of Spacetime Hyperactivity.” Guests can casually relax or dive into activities like investigating clues or playing games, engaging with a trippy, dark academia vibe. Seamlessly integrating visual storytelling with functionality, the Liminal Lodge provides a unique and memorable space for exploration, connection, and engagement.
